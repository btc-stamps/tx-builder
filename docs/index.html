üé® About Bitcoin Stamps

Bitcoin Stamps are immutable digital artifacts stored directly on the Bitcoin blockchain. Unlike other protocols, Stamps data is pruning-resistant, ensuring permanent storage of your digital art and assets.

  • Fully on-chain storage using multisig and P2WSH outputs
  • Pruning-resistant by design
  • Counterparty protocol compatible
  • Support for images, GIFs, and arbitrary data
